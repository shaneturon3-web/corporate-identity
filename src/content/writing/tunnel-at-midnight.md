The tunnel hummed like a held breath. Shane had promised the perimeter would stay READ-ONLY — no clinical surface touched, no patient data in the staging path.

He checked the manifest twice. Static export. Functions bundle. D1 migrations **not** in this deploy.

At the far end, a green pulse: `gate_e: active`.

He exhaled. Some nights the job is not heroics. It is **not** pushing the wrong button.
